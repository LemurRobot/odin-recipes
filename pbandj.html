Learn to Cook: PB and J

  1. Prepare bread, jelly, plate and peanut butter.
  2. Place the bread on the plate.
  3. Spread the peanut butter on the bread.
  4. To the side, place another piece of bread.
  5. Spread the jelly on this slice.
  6. Stack them with the jelly and peanut butter sides touching.
  7. Enjoy!
